January 17, 2023

Changing the colors

Color scheme

  • Default: light
theme:
  theme_style: light

Primary color

  • Default: black
theme:
  colors:
    primary: black

Text color

  • Default: black
theme:
  colors:
    text: black

Title color

  • Default: black
theme:
  colors:
    title: black

Background color

  • Default: white
theme:
  colors:
    background: white
